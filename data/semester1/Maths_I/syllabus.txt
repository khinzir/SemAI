MTH112 - Mathematics I

Course Title: Mathematics I
Course No: MTH112
Nature of Course: Theory
Semester: I
Credit Hours: 3

Full Marks: 80 + 20
Pass Marks: 32 + 8

Course Description

This course covers the concepts of functions, limits, continuity, differentiation, integration of functions of one variable, logarithmic and exponential functions, applications of derivatives and antiderivatives, differential equations, vectors and their applications, partial derivatives, and multiple integrals.

Course Objectives

The objectives of this course are to enable students to:

Understand and formulate real-world problems into mathematical statements.
Develop solutions to mathematical problems at a level appropriate to the course.
Describe or demonstrate mathematical solutions numerically or graphically.
