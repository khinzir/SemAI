CSC109 - Introduction to Information Technology

Course Title: Introduction to Information Technology
Course No: CSC109
Nature of Course: Theory + Lab
Semester: I
Credit Hours: 3

Full Marks: 60 + 20 + 20
Pass Marks: 24 + 8 + 8

Course Description

This course covers the basic concepts of computers and information technology including introduction, hardware, software, memory, input/output, data representation, database, networks and data communication, Internet, multimedia, and computer security.

Course Objectives

The main objective of this course is to provide students with knowledge of fundamental concepts of computers and information technology.
